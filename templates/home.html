Message From The Founder

Hey, I'm Karthik, founder and current president of Smart Minds Tutoring, one of the nation's only free tutoring programs that helps any K-12 student in any main subject area!


When their academics make sense, students leap forward, whether they started out behind or were already excelling. We have helped hundreds of students learn, and we are now ready to bring this progress to you and your child!


Mission Statement

 SMT is a 501(c)(3) Educational Non-Profit. This nonprofit will be formed to provide free tutoring to kids in Elementary, Middle, and High School. Tutors for these students will be merited high school students capable in the field they are teaching. No part of the net earnings of this organization shall inure to the benefit of, or be distributable to, its members, trustees, officers, or other private persons, except that the organization shall be authorized and empowered to pay reasonable compensation for services rendered and to make payments and distributions in furtherance of the purposes set forth in the purpose clause hereof. 

Our Method:

We know how to teach your child most effectively. Our instructors will teach your child how to understand key concepts in an individual setting. Our tutors foster an encouraging environment that helps kids thrive and learn!  


1. We pinpoint your childs learning needs, meet them where they are, and take them where they need to go. 

SMT instructors determine exactly what each child knows and what they need to learn. Our encouraging instructors continually check progress along the way to make sure kids truly understand the concepts weve taught. Kids will see changes in attitude, confidence, and school progress.  


2. And perhaps the part of school none of us liked: homework. 

We will help your child overcome homework frustration. Our instructors will provide homework help by helping kids understand the concepts on the homework assignments. The goal is to help them feel better prepared to complete the work at home while transforming frustration into a welcome challenge. 


3. Money is never a part of SMT. 

SMT is a free, nonprofit tutoring organization, unless you decide to donate money to help us improve our tutoring services, you will never have to pay a dime for us to tutor your children. We instead pay our tutors through service hours, so we got you covered!
